Linear Maps have the following properties:

  1. Parallel lines stay parallel and lines stay lines.
  2. Even spacing is preserved ie: even if the spacing between two points change, the spacing between all points is changed consistently.
  3. The origin is fixed.

A linear map or linear transformation is a mapping between two vector spaces that preserves the operations of vector addition and scalar multiplication.
